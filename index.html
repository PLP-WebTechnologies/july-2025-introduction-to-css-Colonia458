DUOLINGO

Duolingo is a free language-learning app that engages
learners to learn in a unique and fun way.

From its gamified approach to adaptive learning,
Duolingo makes learning easy!

FEATURES

  • XP and Leaderboards
  • Streaks and Lingots
  • Duo the Owl Mascot
  • Level Progression
  • Playful Design

LANGUAGES

Popular languages: Spanish, French, German, Italian, Japanese, Mandarin Chinese
Regional languages: Welsh, Navajo, Hawaiian, Scottish Gaelic
Constructed languages: Esperanto, High Valyrian, Klingon
